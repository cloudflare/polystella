# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in PolyStella welcoming and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for impact.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Give and receive feedback with patience.
- Respect maintainer decisions about scope, priority, and project direction.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, stalking, or threats.
- Insults, slurs, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery where it is not appropriate.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after a maintainer asks you to stop.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make someone feel unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, block accounts, or take other appropriate action when this code of conduct is violated.

If you need to report behavior privately, contact a project maintainer through an appropriate private channel. You can also use GitHub's built-in reporting and moderation tools.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in project spaces, including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and community channels connected to PolyStella.
